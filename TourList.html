Garden of Eden

DAYTRIP

Departing from: Barahona

Horse riding / Duration: 3-4 hours

$55

per adult

Transportation included

Our trek begins in the village Ojeda on teh southern coast of Barahona
We start on a large farm where you only have to stretch out your arm to pick and taste avocados, oranges, lemons, mangos, papayas, sapotes, guavas, passion fruit, and many other types of fruit. +info

After a 2-hours hike upskill, at a mirador(lookou-point) with an amazing panoramic view of the villages Los Patos, Ojeda and Paraiso, the river Riosito, and the high mountains of Cachote. We will continue our morning trek uphill to the village Bario Nuevo, founded by a Haitian family who produces coffee within the cloud forest.

In the afternoon (after lunch), we will cross several coffee farms on top of the mountain and then head all the way downhill to the canyon of the river Riostio. Where the trail meets with the river you can take a refreshing dip in a king-size natural swimming pool, also a popular playground for the local childern. Finally, we will follow the river downhill to the village Paraiso.
