Hispaniola

Caribbean Paradise

Hispaniola is the 22nd-largest islan in the world, located in the Caribbean island group, the Greater Antilles. It is the second largest island in the Caribbean after Cuba, and the thenth most populous island in the world

Hispaniola is the site of the first permanent European settlement in the Americas, founded by Christopher Columbus on his voyages in 1492 and 1493.
